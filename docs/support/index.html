What to include

When asking for support, include the Owlory app version, your device model, the iOS version, and a brief description of what happened.

Please avoid sending sensitive personal notes, reflections, training details, or home routine content unless it is necessary for the support request.

Privacy basics

Owlory does not require account sign-in. App data is stored locally on your device. Microphone and speech recognition permissions are optional and are used only for voice note capture and transcription.
