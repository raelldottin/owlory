Privacy Policy

Owlory is a local-first personal planning, reflection, writing capture, training, and home routine app developed by Raell Dottin.

  • Support

Summary

Owlory does not require account sign-in. App data is stored locally on your device. Owlory does not sell personal data and does not use third-party advertising.

Information you add

Owlory helps you keep personal planning entries, reflections, writing captures, training records, and home routines. The app data is the information you choose to enter while using Owlory.

Local storage

Owlory stores app data locally on your device in Owlory's app storage. Owlory does not require an Owlory account to use the app.

Microphone and speech recognition

Microphone and speech recognition permissions are optional. They are used only when you choose to capture a voice note or transcribe speech into text.

You can use Owlory without granting these permissions, and you can manage them in the iOS Settings app.

Sale of personal data and advertising

Owlory does not sell personal data. Owlory does not use third-party advertising.
