## Reading guide

- `native-reviewed`: native or fluent reviewer accepted the value. Translation-quality claim allowed for that key.
- `needs-layout-check`: candidate value (LLM-drafted or otherwise) needs UI/screenshot review. Not a quality claim.
- `needs-product-decision`: reviewer wants product input before accepting.
- `keep-english-term`: brand, format specifier, or loanword kept identical to English on purpose.
- `needs-translation`: no candidate yet; falls back to English placeholder pending native review.
- `reject`: candidate rejected.
